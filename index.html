The Environmental Sensitivity Mapping (ESM) Webtool is a novel decision-support tool for Strategic Environmental Assessment (SEA) and planning processes in Ireland.  
Using over 100 datasets, the ESM Webtool allows you to create plan-specific ‘environmental sensitivity maps’ that identify natural assets and indicate where suitable development locations are within a given region.
